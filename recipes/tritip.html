Coffee Rub Tri-Tip!

This coffee rub tri tip is absolutley delicious. The coffee mixes with the smoke and the garlic in the rub and the results are unbelievable.

1 teaspoon of:

  • Course Sea Salt
  • Instant Coffee Crystals
  • Garlic Powder
  • Smoked Paprika

1/2 teaspoon of:

  • Cracked Black Pepper
  • Crushed Coriander
  • Onion Powder

1/4 teaspoon of:

  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Chilli Powder

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Pull out Tri Tip from the fridge, and let it sit on the counter for about 2 hours, apply rub generously
  2. pre heat smoker to 225*
  3. Throw the Tri Tip on for about 1.5 hours depending on size. I pull mine at about 130* internal temperature
  4. Crank up the heat to 475* and let grill pre heat for 10 mins, covering tri tip in the meantime.
  5. Throw the meat back on, and sear each side for about 3.5 minutes. (For added style points use a cast iron skillet.)
  6. Pull Tri Tip, loosley cover, let rest for a MINIMUM of 6 minutes.
